Summer Music Festival 2025

June 15-17, 2025
Central Park, New York
2:00 PM - 11:00 PM

About the Event

Get ready for the most electrifying music experience of the year! The Summer Music Festival 2025 returns to Central Park with an incredible lineup of world-renowned artists, emerging talents, and unforgettable performances across multiple stages.

This three-day extravaganza will feature genres spanning from rock and pop to electronic and hip-hop, ensuring there's something for every music lover. With state-of-the-art production, immersive art installations, and gourmet food vendors, this is more than just a concert—it's a complete sensory experience.

Join over 50,000 music enthusiasts as we celebrate the power of music under the summer sky. From sunset performances to late-night DJ sets, every moment promises to be magical.
